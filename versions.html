Alternate versions of A Gentle Introduction to the Art of Mathematics, version 3.2

      

The 'S' flag indicates that the symbol used for logical negation is ~.

The 'N' flag indicates that the convention that 1 (not 0) is the smallest natural number is maintained throughout.
